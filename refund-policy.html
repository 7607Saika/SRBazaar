Refund Policy

The Refund policy offered on #Bazaar is a plan provided directly by the individual sellers.

The refund policy is not applicable on all categories of products. In order to get more details, about the applicability of the refund on specific categories, you can visit here. (Include the link to #Bazaar Return Policy).

Once your returned product is received by the seller, they inform #Bazaar about the receipt. Then a refund is initiated to the original payment method. In case of the payment made via Cash on Delivery, the amount is added to your #Bazaar Wallet.

If a product cannot be repaired or replaced, the seller will issue a refund for the damaged / defective item.
