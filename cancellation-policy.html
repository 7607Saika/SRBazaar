Cancellation Policy

HashTagBazaar (#Bazaar) recognizes that there might be situations, where you need to cancel an order you had placed with us. You have the option to cancel the items or orders by going to the My Order section under My Account.

If your order hasnâ€™t been dispatched:

When you cancel an item or order for a single product, and if you have already made the payment, a refund option is provided to you. You can choose to get the amount being added to your HashTagBazaar Wallet or be returned to you via NEFT.

If your order has already been dispatched:

When you cancel an item, which has already been picked-up from the seller and is in transit then you can cancel the order and if the payment has already been made, a refund of the amount will be provided to you. If you are still contacted by the logistics person, you can refuse to accept the delivery of the item or product.

If your order has already been delivered:

After you have received the delivery of the product, then the cancellation is not possible. Instead, you can choose the option of Return in order to get a refund of the amount paid by you.
