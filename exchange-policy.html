Part 2 - Returns Pick-Up and Processing Rules

The item, which you want to be picked-up for return, exchange or refund needs to adhere to the following and will be checked for the same while being picked-up.

Particulars Condition to be verified
Product Verification IMEI / name / image / brand / serial number / article number / bar code should be the same and the MRP tag should be present and clearly visible.
Product & Accessories All the accessories, any freebies and / or combos shipped with the product (like remote-control, instruction manual, charger, battery, headphone etc.) must be present in the product box, along with the product.
Product State The product must not have been used, must be unwashed, unsoiled, not having any stains and must not have been tampered with. The quality check seal return tags and wherever applicable the warranty seals must be intact. In case of return of a mobile / laptop / tablet, the device should have been restored to factory setting or formatted without any screen lock (Fingerprint, Facial Recognition, Password, Pin or Pattern). The product should not be damaged and should be without any dents, tears, scratches, or holes, including the accessories and components shipped with it.
Product Packaging The original box or packaging in which the product was shipped must not be damaged.

The logistic partner representative will refuse to accept the return if any of the above conditions are not met.

For any products returned for a refund, the refund will be processed once the returned product has been received by the seller.

Part 3 - General Rules for a successful Return

In cases, where the seller is unable to process an exchange for any reason whatsoever, a refund will be given to the customer.

For any products where installation is provided by the brand service partners, do not open the product packaging by yourself. The brand’s authorized personnel will help in unboxing and installation of the product.

In case of Wrong Delivery where the customer received the delivery message, but the product was not delivered, kindly report the issue within 7 days from the date mentioned in the message to SR Bazaar.

Items sold in packs, as sets or combos cannot be exchanged or returned individually.

For Cash on Delivery orders, refunds will be processed either to your bank account (via National Electronic Funds Transfer (NEFT)) or SR Bazaar Wallet.
